Something Good

Web platform for posting and getting information about volunteering events

Idea

The initial idea was to build a web application that would promote community engagement by creating a platform where people can post and get information on volunteering events in Nazareth, Israel. The volunteering would be based on a one time commitment in a variety of activity-types and locations. Users would be able to vote for the volunteering event they like, and each week, one of the events will take place in the time and location that was set.
